Here are Answers to Some Frequently Asked Questions

How does the community make its money?

In all sincerity, honesty and integrity, the community does not make money for itself. Every member of the community including the Admins also participate in the scheme and do the same things and follow the same process, steps and procedures like every other member of the community. It's absolutely transparent and everyone sees it clearly as they move through the boards. It's a wonderful modification of what the African call Osusu.

How much will I gift to a member of the community and how much will I be gifted with?

You gift a member in the collection position #50,000 only and you will be gifted #200,000 by 4 members when you get to the collection position.

Can I be on more than one spot on the same board at the same time?

Yes, only if you have the capacity to introduce more trusted individuals who has integrity. Note: 1 position requires you introduce 2 individuals while 2 positions requires you to introduce 4 individuals and so on

What is the maximum number of boards one can be in at the same time?

Limitless as long as you have the capacity to introduce trusted family and friends who has integrity.

What happens if I register and do not get my 2 people to come in?

You are allowed a maximum of 10 weeks only to introduce your trusted member else someone else takes your spot to move the board forward. And there is no refund.
NOTE: No refund. But you can appeal for a 2 weeks extension and it might be considered if the members in the board agree to it. All decisions are from the members. Admin only ensure adherence to the structure and prosess.

How long after registration do I have to bring in my 2 people before forfeiting my money?

10 to 12 weeks from the registration.

What if my 2 people refuse to bring in their 2 people?

This will stall the board and they have 10 to 12 weeks before they are replaced member. The board has to move on.

NOTE: No refund

How long has the company been in existence?

First of all, it's not a company. It's a plain level transparent platform for forward thinking individuals coming together to gift members and in turn be gifted.
It's been in existence since 2009 in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Europe and Africa in various forms.

What is the name of the owner of the company?

There's no owner, no CEO, no central account, no interference with funds or payments in anyway.
There's only the Admin who rollout boards and ensure compliance with the structure and processes.

Is the company registered with the relevant government bodies?

No. It's not a company in the first place. It's not for the public or random individuals.
It only for family and friends with integrity.
